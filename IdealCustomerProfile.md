Customer Segment: Year 7–12 High School Student (Australia)
Demographics

Age: 12–18 years old
Location: Australia (all states/territories)
Education: Currently enrolled in secondary school (public or private)
Year levels: 7–12, with higher urgency in Years 10–12 (approaching HSC/VCE/QCE etc.)

Pain Points

Struggling with poor or declining grades
Doesn't know how to study effectively
Dealing with fixed mindsets and lack of drive or motivation
Mental and psychological barriers that hold them back academically
Strained relationships with parents/guardians due to academic stress and pressure
Disconnected from their own goals and life prospects
Feels like they're navigating school alone — wishes they had a dedicated guide who understands their specific situation, assessments, and exams
Anxious and uncertain about career direction after school

Desired Outcomes

Noticeably improved grades and results
A better approach to preparing for assessments and exams — practically and mentally
Shift from a fixed mindset to a growth mindset
Get into their dream course and university
Feel a renewed sense of direction, confidence, and purpose

Secondary Customer (Key Influencer)

Parents/guardians — often searching for solutions and funding them, motivated by reducing their child's stress and securing their future
